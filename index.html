Al-Sabeel: A Performance-Driven SaaS Productivity Dashboard

I architected and developed the entire app, demonstrating expertise in building complex, user-centric solutions from concept to completion. Tech Stack: Next.js 15, Sanity.io CMS, NextAuth, TypeScript, ShadCN UI, TailwindCSS.

Tourvisto: Unified Travel Management: A Next.js SaaS Application

Tourvisto is an enterprise-grade SaaS platform engineered to solve inefficiency in the travel industry by unifying client and admin operations into one system. As the sole architect and developer, I delivered: * A secure, dual-role architecture via NextAuth.js middleware. * An intuitive booking portal for travelers. * A powerful admin dashboard with real-time data analytics. This project showcases my expertise in the end-to-end development of complex, data-driven, and scalable applications. Tech: Next.js 15, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Recharts.
